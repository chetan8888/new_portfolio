  • Category: Real Estate
  • Client: NAAD Builders
  • Project date: November 2020

CRM and Property Bookings Web Application

Created a Full Stack web application that consists of two parts. The first part is for the brokers and on-site employees of the agency which allowed them to create bookings on behalf of customerts, schedule site visits with potential customers and track status of any property in a particular project. The second part is specifically designed and developed for the high level management of the company which includes multiple dashboards that track the performance of employees, number of projects in a site, number of bookings in a project and also maintains the data of customers and leads.
